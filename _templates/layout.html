How to Easily Reinstall QuickBooks on a New PC: A Step-by-Step Guide for 2015 and 2009 Versions

Reinstalling QuickBooks on a new PC may seem daunting at first, but with the right steps, it can be done quickly and efficiently. Whether you’re looking to reinstall QuickBooks 2015 or QuickBooks 2009, this guide will help you through the process smoothly. In this article, we'll go over how to reinstall QuickBooks on your new computer, covering topics like downloading QuickBooks, managing license information, and handling common issues.


Get Started With Quickbooks

Why You Need to Reinstall QuickBooks

When switching to a new PC, you’ll need to reinstall QuickBooks to keep your financial data, preferences, and configurations intact. This process will also ensure that QuickBooks functions correctly with your new computer's system. If you’ve already purchased QuickBooks, you can download and reinstall it on the new system without needing to repurchase it.

Step 1: Gather Your QuickBooks License Information

Before you begin, make sure you have your QuickBooks license information handy. This includes:

  • Your Product Number and License Number, which are usually provided during your original purchase or can be found in your QuickBooks account if you registered the product.

If you're unsure where to find these details, check your original purchase email or your QuickBooks account. For versions like QuickBooks 2015 and 2009, this step is especially important to avoid any issues during installation.

Step 2: Download QuickBooks from Your Account

The next step is to download QuickBooks on your new PC. Here’s how you can do that for QuickBooks 2015 or 2009:

  1. Log into Your QuickBooks Account: Visit the official QuickBooks website and log into your QuickBooks account using your registered email and password.
  2. Locate Your QuickBooks Version: Once logged in, find the "Downloads and Updates" section in your account dashboard. If you’ve already purchased QuickBooks, you’ll see your past purchases here.
  3. Download QuickBooks: Select the version you want to reinstall—whether it's QuickBooks 2015 or QuickBooks 2009—and follow the prompts to download the installer to your new PC.

If you encounter any issues with downloading, you may want to check if there’s a "Quicken download already purchased" option, especially if you're also using Quicken. This ensures you’re not getting confused between QuickBooks and Quicken.

You may face issues like "Quicken download already purchased error," which can often be resolved by contacting support or checking if you have the correct system requirements.

QuickBooks vs. Quicken: What’s the Difference?

QuickBooks and Quicken are both financial management software, but QuickBooks is primarily designed for businesses, while Quicken is intended for personal finance management. If you're looking to download Quicken for Windows, ensure you are downloading the right version for your needs. You can download Quicken from my account using the same steps as outlined above for QuickBooks.

Step 3: Install QuickBooks on Your New PC

Once the download is complete, you’ll need to install QuickBooks. Here’s a breakdown of how to install QuickBooks 2015 or 2009 on Windows:

  1. Run the Installer: Double-click the downloaded QuickBooks installer file to begin the installation process.
  2. Accept the License Agreement: You’ll be prompted to accept the license terms. Read through the agreement and click Agree to continue.
  3. Enter Your License Information: During installation, you’ll be asked to input your Product Number and License Number. Make sure to have these details ready. If you purchased QuickBooks previously, you should already have this information on hand.
  4. Choose the Installation Type: Depending on your needs, you can choose either a Typical Installation (for a standard setup) or a Custom Installation (for more control over where and how QuickBooks is installed).
  5. Complete the Installation: Once you’ve chosen your settings, the installation process will begin. Follow the on-screen prompts to finish installing QuickBooks on your new PC.

Step 4: Restore Your Backup (If Applicable)

If you have a backup of your QuickBooks data from your old PC, you can now restore it to the newly installed QuickBooks version.

  1. Open QuickBooks on your new PC after installation.
  2. Go to File > Open or Restore Company.
  3. Choose Restore a Backup Copy and select your backup file (this could be a QuickBooks Backup (.QBB) file).
  4. Follow the prompts to restore your backup.

This step ensures that all your company data, transactions, and reports from your previous installation are transferred over to the new PC.

Step 5: Activate Your QuickBooks Software

After installation, you may need to activate QuickBooks to ensure that your software is genuine and properly licensed.

  1. Launch QuickBooks: Open QuickBooks on your new PC.
  2. Activate: If prompted, enter your license and product numbers again to activate the software.
  3. Complete Setup: Follow any additional setup prompts to finalize the process.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Sometimes, reinstalling QuickBooks can lead to issues such as licensing errors or installation problems. Below are some common issues and their solutions:

1. Quicken Download Already Purchased Error

If you encounter an issue with a Quicken download already purchased error, this could be related to a mismatch in account details or system compatibility. Here’s how to fix it:

  • Verify your account details: Make sure you're logged into the correct QuickBooks or Quicken account.
  • Check for updates: Sometimes an outdated version can cause this issue. Ensure you're downloading the latest updates for QuickBooks.
  • Contact customer support: If the issue persists, contacting QuickBooks support can help you resolve the issue quickly.

2. Reinstalling Quicken: How Do I Get a Replacement Copy of Quicken?

If you need a replacement copy of Quicken (for instance, if you lost your download link), you can reach out to Quicken customer service. They can help you download and reinstall Quicken from your account.

3. Reinstall QuickBooks on Windows 10 or Windows 11

If you’re using Windows 10 or Windows 11 and having trouble reinstalling QuickBooks, make sure the system meets QuickBooks’ minimum system requirements. For compatibility issues, always download QuickBooks for Windows 10 or Windows 11 versions from your account to ensure smooth installation.

Step 6: Final Checks

After completing the installation and restoration, make sure that:

  • Your company file is correctly restored.
  • You can access all the features of QuickBooks.
  • Your financial data is intact.

If you face any issues or glitches, restarting your computer or reinstalling QuickBooks might help resolve minor installation issues.

Be sure to have your product and license numbers handy, download QuickBooks from your account, and restore your backup file to continue managing your finances seamlessly.
